French Fries Recipe

Description

French Fries is a popular dish made from potatoes. They are popularly eaten in fast food restaurants, accompanied by Chicken or Burgers

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon garlic powder
  • Vegetable oil

Cooking Instructions

  1. Cut the potatoes using a serrated knife into thin slices
  2. Soak the potatoes in cold water with salt for about half an hour
  3. Drain the potatoes and pat them to dry
  4. Heat the oil to 300 degrees
  5. Fry the potatoes in batches until complete
  6. Sprinkle salt and garlic powder on the fries to taste
