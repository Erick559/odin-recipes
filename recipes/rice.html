Rice Recipe

Description

Rice is a vegetarian dish that is often served in a variety of cuisines. It can be served in a variety of ways, including in a salad, in a bowl, in a dish, or in a meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup salt
  • 2 cups of water

Cooking Instructions

  1. Wash the rice until the water runs clear
  2. Add the 2 cups of water to a pot and bring it to a boil
  3. Add the salt and the drained rice
  4. After a couple of minutes lower the heat and let the rice simmer
  5. If all the water evaporates remove the pot from the heat and fluff the rice
